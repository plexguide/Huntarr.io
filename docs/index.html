What is Huntarr?

Huntarr runs alongside your existing Sonarr, Radarr, Lidarr, Readarr, Whisparr, and Prowlarr instances. It continuously searches for missing media, upgrades existing files to better quality, and manages swap-outs via Swaparr — all automatically and on a schedule you control.

Beyond the *arr apps, Huntarr also includes Movie Hunt (a standalone movie grabber with its own indexers and download clients), NZB Hunt (Usenet search), and Requests (a media request system for the person running Huntarr — not for other users).

Key Features

Automated Hunting

Continuously searches for missing TV shows, movies, music, books, and more across all your *arr instances.

Quality Upgrades

Automatically upgrades existing media to higher quality when better releases become available.

Smart Scheduling

Per-app, per-instance schedules with configurable cycles, intervals, and caps so you never overload your indexers.

Notifications

Discord, Telegram, Pushover, Email, Apprise, and more. Know the moment Huntarr finds or grabs something.

Movie Hunt

A full standalone movie grabber with indexer support, download client integration, and custom format scoring.

Swaparr Integration

Automatically manage stalled or slow downloads by swapping them for better alternatives.

Requests

Discover and request movies and shows for your library — for the person running Huntarr, not for other users.

Secure & Self-Hosted

Runs on your own hardware. Authentication, 2FA, and Plex SSO keep your setup safe.
